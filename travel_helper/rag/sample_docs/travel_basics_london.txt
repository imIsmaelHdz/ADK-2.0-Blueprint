Travel basics: London (quick reference)

1) Heathrow (LHR) to central London
- Elizabeth line: fast, modern, typically the simplest option to reach Paddington / Tottenham Court Road.
- Piccadilly line: cheaper but slower; good if you're staying along the Piccadilly line.
- Taxi/ride-hail: expensive and traffic-dependent.

2) Common payment tips
- Contactless card payments are widely accepted on public transport and in shops.
- Keep a small amount of cash for edge cases, but most travelers can go cashless.

3) Etiquette and practical notes
- Stand on the right on escalators; walk on the left.
- In many pubs you order at the bar, not at the table.

4) Weather (general guidance)
- London weather changes quickly; a light rain jacket is often more useful than an umbrella.
